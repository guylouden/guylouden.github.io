Preppers is a series of related exhibitions by artists Guy Louden, Dan McCabe, and Loren Kronemyer. Preppers are a subculture of people that actively prepare for the collapse of society. They collect specialised survival products, skills, and knowledge.
 
Prepping has developed its own codified aesthetics, vocabulary, and consumer objects that embody its apocalyptic and individualistic values. They imagine a post-apocalyptic future without society or state, or in prepper jargon, a world ‚ÄúWithout Rule of Law‚Äù. Among the rich, prepping has ironically led to a boom in luxury disaster provisions and gear. When the tables turn, the prepared wish to emerge with every advantage.
 
This project was funded by the Department of Local Government, Sport and Cultural Industries (DLGSC) Culture and the Arts (WA).
