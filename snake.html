GUY LOUDEN

I am an Australian artist and curator living in Fremantle/ Wayalup and born in Toronto. I have run and founded experimental galleries and curated major exhibitions. My artwork has been exhibited in Sydney, Melbourne, Perth, regionally and online.

Snake, 2020
Videogame, live financial data.

Play

A remake of the classic Snake videogame, but as critique of technocapitalism. It adopts the mythic symbol of the snake that eats its own tail, and pulls live financial data and news from startups.

Playable in-browser on desktops and also on custom artist-made hardware.
